# sales-performance-analysis
# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard — Excel

An interactive sales analytics dashboard built in Microsoft Excel, analyzing 4 years of US retail orders data (2014–2017) across regions, categories, and customer segments.

![Dashboard Preview](screenshots/dashboard_preview.png)

---

## 🔍 Overview

This project analyzes the popular **Superstore Sales Dataset** to uncover trends in revenue, profit, returns, and customer behavior across the United States.

The workbook includes everything from raw data to a fully interactive dashboard with slicers and dynamic charts.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- **KPI Cards** — Total Sales, Net Sales, Profit, COGS, Discounts, Returns, No. of Orders, No. of Customers
- **Sales by Category** — Pie chart comparing Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology
- **Sales by Region** — Breakdown across Central, East, South, and West
- **Sales by Sub-Category** — Top performing sub-categories
- **Top 10 Cities in Sales** — Bar chart of highest revenue cities
- **Top 10 States in Sales** — Bar chart of highest revenue states
- **Sales by States & Categories** — Clustered bar chart
- **Sales & Quantity by Year** — Trend line from 2014 to 2017
- **Interactive Slicers** — Filter by State, Person, Category, and Returned

## 🗂️ Workbook Structure

| Sheet | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `Orders` | Raw data — 9,994 orders with sales, profit, discount, shipping info |
| `Shipping Cost` | Shipping cost per unit by US state |
| `Return` | List of returned orders |
| `People` | Sales representatives by region |
| `PIVOT Calculation` | Intermediate pivot calculations |
| `SALES pivots` | Pivot tables powering the dashboard |

## 📦 Dataset

- **Source:** Superstore Sales Dataset (widely used in data analysis practice)
- **Records:** 9,994 orders
- **Period:** 2014 – 2017
- **Region:** United States
- **Fields:** Order ID, Order Date, Ship Mode, Customer, Segment, City, State, Region, Category, Sub-Category, Product, Quantity, Sales, Discount, Profit, COGS

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Total Sales:** $2,220,163 across 4,855 orders
- **Top Region:** West leads in sales volume
- **Top City:** New York City with $243,398 in sales
- **Top State:** California with $447,421 in sales
- **Profit Margin:** ~12% overall
- **Sales Trend:** Consistent growth from 2014 to 2017
